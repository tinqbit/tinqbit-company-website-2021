Our Projects

Naadanvipani is an online platform launched in collaboration with the agricultural dept. of Pathanamthita district, Horticorp, VFPCK and Govt.of Kerala. This is an easily accessible platform finding solutions to the queries of both farmers and common people. It is an online platform where one can buy fresh vegetables and commodities.
